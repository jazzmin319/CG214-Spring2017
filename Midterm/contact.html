WELCOME


My name is Jazzmin Santiago. I am a fourth semester photography student at the Fashion Institute of Technology.

About My Work:


I grew up in New York City. Living in a big city such as New York, I've always grown up living life with a wild imagination. I use the city as my canvas to paint a lifestyle through the pictures I take. Although New York City is known as a competitive and angry city, I've learned to portrait the city as a warm home.
