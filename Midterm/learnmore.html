Childhood

Growing up in New York City subtly fueled me wanting to be a photographer. I've always seen my city a real life movie waiting to be filmed by everyone's eyes. Being raised by parent's who had grown up in different cultures merging a blended Latin family, I have memories that have been snapshot and saved into my memory for years. My maternal grandmother had been living in Red Hook all my life when I started taking the train to school. I can always see the train coming up the tracks heading toward Coney Island as I'd wait for a downtown F train.


New York has always been a canvas for my work. After taking the downtown F train from Red Hook, we'd always go home to our apartment near the navy yard in Farragut Public Housing. When I'd look out my window I could imagine these beautiful pictures of Manhattan's skyline in the skies.
